What is Odyssey?

Odyssey is a new project built upon Commonwealth P3C - a Planetary Prosperity for the Future. It offers an alternative interface to manage your Points and Dividends, and will offer more unique features as development continues.

Odyssey has been developed atop the Pure model of P3C - so you will have tokens directly in your wallet, rather than in a Farm or Crop. If you wish to use these features, you can access them via The original Commonwealth interface.
